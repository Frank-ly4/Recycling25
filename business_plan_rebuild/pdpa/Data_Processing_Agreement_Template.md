# Data Processing Agreement (DPA) — Template (Thailand PDPA)

**This DPA** is intended for use by Recycling25 Co., Ltd. as **Data Controller** with a vendor acting as **Data Processor** (e.g., cloud storage, CRM, accounting, driver log app).

---

## 1. Parties
- **Controller:** Recycling25 Co., Ltd. (“Controller”)
- **Processor:** [Vendor legal name] (“Processor”)

## 2. Scope and term
- **Services:** [describe the service]
- **Start date / term:** [date] until termination of the services, plus any required transition period.

## 3. Processing details
- **Subject matter:** Processing of personal data necessary to deliver the services.
- **Duration:** For the term of the services.
- **Nature of processing:** Collection, storage, access, retrieval, deletion, transmission as required.
- **Purposes:** Service delivery, reporting, SLA measurement, compliance, and support.
- **Data categories:** Contact details; service verification logs; operational photos; billing admin data (as applicable).
- **Data subjects:** Client representatives; employees/contractors; others incidentally captured (minimized).

## 4. Controller instructions
- Processor shall process personal data **only on documented instructions** from Controller, including regarding transfers.

## 5. Confidentiality
- Processor ensures personnel with access are bound by confidentiality and trained on PDPA/security.

## 6. Security measures
Processor will implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including at minimum:
- Access controls (least privilege), MFA for admin access
- Encryption in transit; encryption at rest where feasible
- Logging and monitoring of access
- Secure backups and recovery
- Segregation of customer data (where applicable)

## 7. Sub-processing
- Processor may not appoint sub-processors without Controller’s written authorization (general or specific).
- Processor remains fully liable for sub-processor performance.

## 8. Assistance with data subject rights
- Processor will assist Controller to respond to data subject requests within a reasonable time.

## 9. Personal data breach notification
- Processor will notify Controller **without undue delay** after becoming aware of a personal data breach, providing:
  - nature of breach, categories/approx counts
  - likely consequences
  - mitigation actions taken/proposed
  - contact point for follow-up

## 10. Cross-border transfers
- Processor will not transfer personal data outside Thailand except as instructed and with PDPA-compliant safeguards.

## 11. Deletion/return at end of services
- Upon termination, Processor will return or securely delete personal data per Controller instruction, unless retention is required by law.

## 12. Audit and compliance
- Processor will make available information necessary to demonstrate compliance and allow reasonable audits (including third-party reports where appropriate).

## 13. Liability
- [Define liability cap, exclusions, and indemnities consistent with the master services agreement.]

## 14. Priority and governing law
- This DPA is incorporated into the relevant services agreement; in case of conflict, this DPA governs data protection terms.
- Governing law: Kingdom of Thailand.
