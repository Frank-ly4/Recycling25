# Investor QA Checklist (Due Diligence Defense)

**Purpose:** This list anticipates the "hard questions" sophisticated investors will ask during diligence and points to the specific document or evidence that answers them.

## 1. Commercial & Market
*   [ ] **"Is the 90 THB/unit fee realistic?"**
    *   *Answer:* Validated via [Pilot Validation Plan](audits/pilot_validation.md). Pricing stress-tested in [Sensitivity Analysis](../financials/sensitivity_analysis.md) (Price -10% scenario).
*   [ ] **"Why won't condos just use free BMA collection?"**
    *   *Answer:* BMA collection is mixed-waste only (60 THB/unit/month fee pressure) or unreliable. We provide the *ESG Audit Trail* (Diversion Report) that Juristic Managers need for corporate reporting. See [ESG Sales Talking Points](../outreach_kit/ESG_sales_talking_points.md).
*   [ ] **"What prevents a price war with informal collectors?"**
    *   *Answer:* Professional insurance, VAT invoices, and digital reporting. Informal collectors cannot provide the "Chain of Custody" required by publicly listed developers (Sansiri/AP).

## 2. Operations & Asset
*   [ ] **"Can your trucks actually fit in older condo basements?"**
    *   *Answer:* Yes. We use a **Mixed Fleet Strategy** (Isuzu NPR for street loading + Suzuki Carry for <2.1m basements). See [Operations Plan](../operations_plan.md) Section 3 and [Vehicle Specs](../audits/vehicle_specs_comparison.md).
*   [ ] **"What happens if your truck breaks down?"**
    *   *Answer:* Our lease with Siam Rajathanee includes a replacement vehicle SLA (24-48 hours).
*   [ ] **"Do you have a license to operate?"**
    *   *Answer:* We file for the BMA "Business Detrimental to Health" permit. For transport, we use the "License Bridge" (leasing via a licensed operator) in Year 1. See [Legal Compliance](../legal_compliance.md).
