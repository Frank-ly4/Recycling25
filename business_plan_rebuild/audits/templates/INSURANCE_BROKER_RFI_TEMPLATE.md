**Purpose:** Get binding quotes fast, with clear coverage limits that match operational risks.

## 1) Business overview (copy/paste)
We operate a scheduled recyclables pickup service for condominium juristic person buildings in Bangkok, transporting source-separated recyclables to a sorting/aggregation point. Operations involve loaders, drivers, on-site collection, and warehouse handling.

## 2) What we need quoted (requested coverage)
Please quote:
- **Commercial vehicle insurance** (compulsory + comprehensive + third-party liability)

- **Property/contents** for warehouse equipment (scales/baler/bins/signage inventory)
