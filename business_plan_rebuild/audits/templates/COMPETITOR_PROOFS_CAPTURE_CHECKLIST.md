# Competitor Proof Capture Checklist (Screenshots + Quotes)

**Purpose:** Turn “competitors exist” into investor-grade evidence.

## 1) What to capture per competitor
- Name + website/app store link
- Stated service scope (materials, pickup schedule)
- Pricing/fee terms (screenshots)
- Coverage area (screenshots)
- Any stated partners/clients (screenshots)
- Date captured

## 2) Evidence storage
- Put screenshots/PDF prints under: `business_plan_rebuild/evidence/market/competitors/` (create subfolder per competitor)
- Include a short `README.md` per competitor with:
  - captured URLs
  - capture date/time
  - 2–4 bullet summary of what the evidence shows

## 3) Update diligence artifacts
- Update `appendices/research_briefs/market/20251215_Competitor_Landscape.md`
- Update `INVESTOR_QA_151225.md` question `CL-02` citations
- Update `evidence_register.json` competitor claim(s)
