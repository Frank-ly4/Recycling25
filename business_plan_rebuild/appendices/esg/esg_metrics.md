# ESG Metrics & Reporting Framework

**Status:** Draft (desk-ready; pilot data will populate)  
**Last updated:** 2026-01-02  
**Owner:** Operations / Governance

## 1) Reporting cadence
- **Monthly (pilot buildings):** diversion + service KPIs (building-level)
- **Quarterly (investor pack):** ESG roll-up + governance + compliance status

## 2) Metric dictionary (investor-grade)

### 2.1 Environmental (E)
| Metric | Definition | Unit | Data source | Collection method | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Total recyclables collected | Total mass collected across all included streams | kg | Scale logs / weighbridge receipts | Aggregate monthly logs | Monthly |
| PET collected | PET mass collected | kg | Scale logs / weighbridge receipts | Material-separated logs | Monthly |
| Contamination rate | Rejected/contaminated material mass ÷ total collected | % | QA checks + processor feedback | QA sampling + receipt reconciliation | Monthly |
| CO₂e avoided (proxy) | \(\sum m_i \times f_i\) using current factors | kgCO₂e | Impact methodology + factors memo | Apply factors to measured weights | Monthly |

### 2.2 Social (S)
| Metric | Definition | Unit | Data source | Collection method | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Direct jobs supported | Headcount employed by Recycling25 for operations | # | Payroll / HR roster | Monthly HR snapshot | Quarterly |
| Safety incidents (recordable) | Count of recordable incidents | # | Incident log | Incident reporting SOP | Monthly |
| Training completion | % of ops staff completing required training | % | Training attendance | Attendance + checklist | Quarterly |
| Informal collector integration (if applicable) | # of informal collectors engaged under defined program terms | # | Partner roster + agreements | Signed terms + payment logs | Quarterly |

### 2.3 Governance (G)
| Metric | Definition | Unit | Data source | Collection method | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Board meeting cadence | # meetings held vs planned | # | Board calendar/minutes | Calendar + minutes archive | Quarterly |
| Regulatory status | BMA/DLT/RD status snapshot | Text | `legal_compliance.md` + evidence folders | Status review + evidence check | Quarterly |
| PDPA incidents | # of reported privacy/security incidents | # | PDPA incident log | Incident reporting | Quarterly |
