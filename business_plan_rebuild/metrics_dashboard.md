# Metrics Dashboard

## 1. North Star Metrics (Monthly)
*   **Net Profit:** Target ≥60,000 THB/month net income (by Month 15 in current base case).
*   **Active Buildings:** Target ~12 (to support ≥60k/month net income in current base case).
*   **Customer Retention:** Target 100%.

## 2. Financial KPIs
*   **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue):** Sum of all Service Fees.
*   **Gross Margin:** (Revenue - Direct Labor/Fuel) / Revenue. Target >50%.
*   **Cash Runway:** Months of cash remaining at current burn rate.

## 3. Operational KPIs
*   **On-Time Collection Rate:** Target ≥98%.
*   **Avg Weight per Building:** Target >200 kg/month (Validates participation).
*   **Contamination Rate:** Target <15% (by weight).

## 4. Sales Pipeline
*   **LOIs Signed:** Count.
*   **Site Audits Completed:** Count.
*   **Conversion Rate:** Audit -> Contract.

## 5. Data Collection Plan
*   **Source:** Driver App / Google Form Log.
*   **Frequency:** Daily (Ops), Monthly (Financials).
*   **Review:** Weekly Management Meeting (Monday 10 AM).
