# Processor Memorandum of Understanding (Template)

Parties: Recycling25 Co., Ltd. (Seller) and [Processor Name] (Buyer)

## 1. Purpose
Formalize quality, pricing floors, logistics, and documentation for recyclable materials.

## 2. Materials and Specifications
- PET (baled): moisture ≤ 5%, contamination ≤ 2%
- HDPE (baled): moisture ≤ 5%, contamination ≤ 3%
- OCC/Cardboard (baled): moisture ≤ 8%, contamination ≤ 3%
- Non-conforming loads may be downgraded or rejected with photo evidence.

## 3. Weighing and Documentation
- Certified scale weight tickets at Buyer site.
- Chain-of-custody records: pickup time, vehicle plate, photos (pre/post), bale IDs.
- Discrepancies documented within 24 hours with photos.

## 4. Pricing and Floors
- Price floors per Schedule A (by material and grade) valid for the calendar month.
- Floor review monthly; Buyer to notify changes ≥3 business days in advance.
- Downgrade schedule per Schedule A applies when specs unmet.

## 5. Logistics
- Delivery/Pickup windows: Mon–Sat, 09:00–17:00 (unless otherwise agreed).
- Turnaround target ≤ 90 minutes per truck.
- Safety and PPE requirements to be provided by Buyer.

## 6. Payment Terms
- Net 15 days from receipt of invoice and weigh tickets.
- Disputes raised within 5 business days; undisputed amounts payable on time.

## 7. Term and Termination
- Term: 12 months from signature; early termination with 30 days’ notice.
- Immediate termination for repeated spec failures or non-payment.

## 8. Audit and Compliance
- Seller may audit quality determinations with 24h notice.
- Parties comply with environmental and labor laws; anti-corruption commitments.

## 9. Contacts
- Operations, Finance, Quality contacts to be exchanged upon execution.
