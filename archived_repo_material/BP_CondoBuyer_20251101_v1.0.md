# Condo/FM Buyer Brief — Bangkok Condo Recycling

Audience: Condo juristic boards and facility managers (Bangkok). Practical emphasis: reliability, compliance, onsite experience.

## What you get
- Weekly recyclable collection; bins/signage; resident notices (EN/TH); monthly ESG report; issue response ≤ 24h.
- Measured reliability with performance credits (see `docs/GoToMarket/05_Pilot_SLA.md`).

## How we work (SLA)
- On-time target ≥ 98% (definitions per `ops/SOPs/04_SLA_Measurement_SOP.md`).
- Credits if we miss thresholds; simple issue reporting via QR; documented chain-of-custody.

## Pricing & packages (building-level)
- Fee per unit/month with clear inclusions; 25/50/75/100% coverage options.
- See `finance/pricing_catalog.csv` for package header/fields; final price in Schedule A.

## Onboarding kit (what you receive)
- Signage and labels; resident notices (EN/TH); QR codes for reporting; SLA summary and definitions.
- Included as Schedule B of the 12-month agreement (`docs/GoToMarket/06_Service_Agreement_12m.md`).

## ROI perspective
- Conservative ROI based on avoided BMA fees and reduced staff burden; see `docs/GoToMarket/ROI_Calculator.md`.
- Upside cases (sponsorship/material credits) only if supported by processor MOUs.

## Compliance & safety
- PDPA policy; insured vehicles; PPE; incident reporting within 24h. Permit path documented in legal references.

## Getting started
1) 30–60 day pilot (2–3 pickups/week as needed) with simple pilot letter.
2) Review monthly ESG dashboard; choose tier and finalize Schedule A.
3) Convert to 12-month agreement with performance credits and onboarding kit.
